Everything you've wondered about how QuiltingCompare.com works — and a few things you probably haven't.

About This Site

What is QuiltingCompare.com?
QuiltingCompare.com is a free price comparison tool for quilt batting. We track prices from Linda's Electric Quilters, Amazon, and Walmart so you can see where the best deal is without opening twelve tabs on your phone while standing in a quilt shop parking lot — which is exactly the situation that created this site.
How often are prices updated?
Prices are updated automatically every day at 5 AM Eastern Time. You'll see the last verified date on each comparison page. Prices on retail sites can change at any time — flash sales, price drops, out-of-stock situations — so we always recommend clicking through to confirm the price before you buy.
Which retailers do you track?
We currently compare prices across three retailers: Linda's Electric Quilters (lindas.com), Amazon (amazon.com), and Walmart (walmart.com). We chose these three because they're the most widely accessible for US quilters and have the broadest batting selection online. We only include official store prices — no third-party marketplace sellers with inflated prices.
Which batting brands do you cover?
We currently track Hobbs Batting, The Warm Company (Warm & Natural), Pellon, Quilters Dream, and Bosal Katahdin. We're adding brands over time based on reader requests — if you'd like to see a brand added, let us know via the newsletter.
Why does a product show "Not listed" for some retailers?
Some batting products aren't carried by every retailer. Linda's specializes in quilting supplies so they carry a wider selection of specialty batting; Walmart carries the more mainstream brands and sizes. "Not listed" means that product simply isn't available at that retailer — it's not out of stock, it's just not sold there.

Affiliate Links & Pricing

What are the affiliate links on this site?
When you click a price and make a purchase, we earn a small commission from the retailer at no extra cost to you. This is called an affiliate commission — it's how we keep the site free and the price updates running daily. We participate in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, the Walmart affiliate program through Impact, and work directly with Linda's Electric Quilters.
Does using affiliate links cost me anything extra?
No. You pay exactly the same price you'd pay going directly to the retailer's website. The commission comes out of the retailer's margin, not yours. Clicking our links is a free way to support a site that saves you time and money.
Why do some prices have a ⚠ warning symbol?
The ⚠ symbol means that price was estimated from search results rather than verified directly from a live product page. Prices without the ⚠ symbol have been confirmed on the retailer's site within the last 24 hours. We always recommend clicking through and confirming before you buy — especially on ⚠ flagged prices.
The price on the site is different from what I see at the store. What happened?
Retail prices change constantly — sometimes multiple times in a day. Our automated update runs at 5 AM ET, so a price may have changed between our update and when you're reading. If you spot a significant discrepancy, we'd love to know — just reply to any of our newsletters and we'll investigate.

The Newsletter & Blog

What's in the Wednesday newsletter?
Every Wednesday we send three recently published blog articles plus a curated list of that week's best batting deals from across the three retailers. Nothing spammy, nothing promotional that we're paid to include — just the content we'd want in our own inbox if we were subscribers.
What do you do with my email address?
We use it exclusively to send you the Wednesday newsletter. We use Flodesk to manage our email list. We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with anyone. Ever. You can unsubscribe at any time with one click and your address is removed immediately.
Do you accept sponsored posts or paid partnerships?
No. Every article on this site is written without payment or influence from batting manufacturers or retailers. Our only revenue is the affiliate commissions you generate by clicking our comparison links. We'll always disclose any change to this policy clearly in our footer.
